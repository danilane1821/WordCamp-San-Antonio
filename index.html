About WordCamp

The beautiful city of San Antonio, TX will be the host city for WordCamp!

Explore the future of WordPress with a lineup of industry professionals. Discover new techniques to advance your career as a web designer.

Expert Speakers

Our expert speaker lineup was just announced, so don't wait too long before grabbing your tickets!

Want to meet the international WordPress community and share skills with some of the world's top experts and makers? Be the first to know what to expect for the future of WordPress.

WordCamp is committed to being inclusive and welcoming for everyone. We look forward to another intensive day of learning and sharing.

Speakers

Jessica Flores

Jessica Flores has been in the Web Development indusrty for 6 years and will be sharing the benefits she has had from using WordPress in the work place. Jessica will also be sharing Keynote: WordPress5.0

Jordan Allen

Jordan Allen has been in the Web Development indusrty for 10 years. Jordan will be demonstrating how to re-vamp any site with WordPress, as well as WooCommerce Basics.

Amanda Smith

Amanda Smith has been in the Web Development industy for 3 years and will be speaking on her favorite aspects of WordPress and how to create your own theme.

Carl Westbrook

Carl Westbrook has been in the Web Development indusrty for 12 years and will be speaking on how he developes plugins and how he uses them within WordPress.

Heather Applegate

Heather Applegate has been in the Web Development industry for 8 years and will be speaking on her favorite ways to come up with new fresh content for WordPress.

Tony Gates

Tony Gates has been in the Web Development indusrty for 3 years and will be explaining the importance of a contect managment system. He will also explore WPEngine and Flywheel as well.
